# TeacherOS 📚🤖

> **Less Paperwork. More Teaching.**

TeacherOS is an AI-powered teaching companion designed to help Nigerian primary and secondary school teachers plan lessons faster, improve classroom delivery, and stay aligned with the latest curriculum. The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to digitize handwritten lesson notes, generate curriculum-aligned lesson plans, create assessments, and provide real-time teaching support tailored to Nigerian classrooms.

Our mission is to reduce teacher workload while improving teaching quality and student learning outcomes.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Despite curriculum reforms in Nigeria, many teachers still face challenges such as:

* Spending hours preparing lesson notes manually
* Limited access to continuous professional development
* Difficulty keeping up with curriculum updates
* Overcrowded classrooms with limited teaching resources
* Heavy administrative workload that reduces teaching effectiveness

These challenges contribute to teacher burnout, inconsistent lesson delivery, and lower student engagement.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

TeacherOS acts as an intelligent AI co-teacher by helping educators:

* 📸 Digitize handwritten lesson notes using OCR
* 📝 Generate structured, curriculum-aligned lesson plans
* 🤖 Ask an AI teaching assistant for lesson support
* 📚 Generate quizzes, assessments, and marking guides
* ✅ Validate lessons against the NERDC curriculum
* 💡 Receive modern pedagogical recommendations
* 📄 Export lesson plans to PDF or Word
* 📱 Access teaching resources on affordable Android devices

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📸 Smart Lesson Note Digitization

Convert handwritten lesson notes into editable digital lesson plans using OCR technology.

### 🤖 AI Lesson Plan Generator

Generate complete lesson plans including:

* Learning Objectives
* Teaching Materials
* Classroom Activities
* Assessment Questions
* Lesson Summary

### 💬 AI Teaching Assistant

An intelligent chatbot that helps teachers:

* Explain difficult concepts
* Generate classroom activities
* Suggest creative teaching methods
* Recommend differentiated learning strategies

### 📚 Curriculum Alignment

Automatically validates lessons against the Nigerian (NERDC) curriculum to ensure teachers remain up to date.

### 📝 Assessment Generator

Create:

* Objective Questions
* Theory Questions
* Marking Guides
* Practice Exercises

### 📄 Export & Sharing

Export lesson plans to PDF or Word documents and share them with colleagues.

### 📶 Offline-First Support

Designed for low-connectivity environments with local storage and synchronization when internet access becomes available.
